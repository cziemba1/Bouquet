La dolce vitta garden wedding

A magical wedding in the Temperate House at Kew Gardens – the World’s largest remaining Victorian Greenhouse. For our couple Yina and Dan from New York, they held their personalised ceremony at the Nash conservatory beneath a cascading tree of epic proportions.

With a drinks reception to follow amongst the tropical plants at the Princess of Wales Conservatory, before guests were lead into the Temperate House. They were greeted by long banquet table dining beneath cathedrals of magical lighting.
